Color-Scheme References:

Body:

A lighter color scheme was chosen for the body to make everything on top stand out. I chose #16BDF0 for the body of the page background, it is a really light blue to go with a weather theme and to make the rest of the page stand out.

Header/Footer:

For the header and the footer I chose a darker shade of blue and made those the same for consistency in the page. I wanted to colors to match the logo as well. The color for the header and footer is #0997D9

Type:

The type is white, to make it stand out throughout the page. I also added a lighter green for the hoover to also match the logo.
