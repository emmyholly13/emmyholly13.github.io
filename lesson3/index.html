Site Purpose:

The purpose for this site is to provide the audience with information about the local weather. This site will provide a quick way to see what the weather will be where you are headed, wether it is in your car, traveling, biking, or just out for a walk!

It also provides information about wether or not you can go out and enjoy a bike ride in the current conditions.
