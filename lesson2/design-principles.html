Visual Hierarchy

Tepui

tepui.com

Visual Hierarchy is when something visually is the focal point and other items follow that focal point creating a bigger object which is the Hierarchy on the page.

In this mobile view, we see the main top image is the focal point in the page, and the other images below that are smaller but part of the page, giving the page the Hierarchy principle look.

Alignment

Adobe

adobe.com

Alignment in design principles is when some sort of alignment is created to arrage things on a page and have it look appealing to the eye.

In this mobile view for Adobe's webpage, we see how they left-aligned text throughout their page giving it that consistant look throughout the page and making it visually appealing.

Repetition

Apple

apple.com

Repetition is a very important in the design process of a website. It gives the viewer familarity in the page, and makes the page consistant with colors, logo, and other branding aspects.

Apple does a great job making their page consistant with positioning their apple logo throughout the page, and maintaining the same font colors across their site.
